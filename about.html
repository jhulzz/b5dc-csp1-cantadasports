Meet the Team

We are a family of sports enthusiasts and athletes. Our aim is to share with you the passion and love for sports and recreational activities. The Center is managed by the Cantada family headed by Edgardo “Tito Boy” Cantada, brother of Joe Cantada. His only two children are both athletes and sports achievers.

Gerard Cantada

He is currently the Deputy Secretary of Philippine Volleyball Federation. He is the Tournament Director in the 11th Philippines International Junior Golf Championship 2011. Gerard was a former member of the Philippines Junior Golf Team to various international competitions.

Contact Gerard

Therese Cantada

As a member of several national teams for world events, Therese gained numerous awards as a record holder in the Air Pistol event and a consistent medalist during the SEA and Asian games.

Contact Therese

Edgardo "Tito Boy" Cantada

Managing Director of Cantada Sports. He is also currently the President of the Philippine Volleyball Federation. He is the brother of the late Smokin' Joe Cantada.

Contact Tito Boy
