Take a Tour on our Facilities

Play like a Pro! Play Basketball, Beach Volleyball, Boxing, Air Pistol/Gun Shooting, Golf, Swimming, Badminton or Table Tennis at our top-notch facilities.

The place to be! Create lasting memories of your sports fests, tournaments, friendly games, team buildings, birthday/Christmas parties, reunions, trainings, camps, sports clinics or retreats at our quality sports areas surrounded by a safe and friendly environment.

Don Ambrosio Indoor Basketball Gym

Basketball is one of the well-loved, if not the most, sports in the Philippines and all over the world. Our basketball gym is our main centerpiece. Fully air-conditioned, complete with locker rooms, showers and bleachers, it can house around 1000 spectators.

Beach Volleyball Court

Do your most powerful volleyball spike attacks at the only lighted and all-weather beach volleyball court in the country. It has a specially-designed drainage system patterned after sand traps found in golf courses. A lightning detector same as those used in US football fields and golf courses alerts players of any lightning activity. Rental Rate: Php 1,000 per hour (without use of lights)

Firing Range

Do you want to know where to practice target shooting? Join Cantada Sports Gun Club and learn the rules of gun safety and shooting basics. Being armed is more than pulling the trigger and experiencing an outright adrenaline rush through your veins. It is about protection, safety and discipline.

15-mtr. Shooting Range

Responsible gun safety is required at all times.

Outdoor Dining Area

Who wouldn't want an al fresco area fronting a beach volleyball court? This is where we usually place our guests especially when they request for a buffet type dining. A perfect venue for catching up with your friends and loved ones!

Conference or Function Rooms

A place to relax, dine and settle down. The center has a multi-purpose, air-conditioned social hall. It also has numerous function rooms, dining areas, a first-class cocktail lounge and complete restaurant facilities.

Lodging

Staying in has never been so comfortable. Our lodging rooms are complete with everything that you need – a refrigerator, television and water heater.

Dipping Pool

Plunge in and cool down to our mini pool after an intense beach volleyball game!
